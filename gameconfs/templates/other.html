Other event directories

If you're looking for game jams, compos, tournaments, festivals and concerts, Gameconfs is not the right site. But you might find what you're looking for here:

  • CompoHub
  • Zo-ii's event blog
  • Game Jam Central
  • NerdyConcerts.com, your one-stop source for all video game, anime, cartoon and movie related concerts.
  • Tournament Seeker, a directory of video game tournaments.
  • Promoter Calendar, a curated festival and competition calendar for independent game developers.
  • Game Convention Central, a directory of board game and pen and paper RPG conventions.
  • GamingEvents.co.uk, a directory of events in the UK with a focus on retro and classic gaming.
  • Pervasive Games and Playful Things, a Google calendar for pervasive and playful events.
  • London Game Diary, a list of upcoming London game events, exhibitions, meetups and more.

(These sites are not associated with Gameconfs or vice versa.)
