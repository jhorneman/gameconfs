GAMES BRITANNIA: Schools Videogame Festival

Magna Science Adventure Centre, Templeborough
Mon 2nd Jul - Sun 8th Jul
www.gamesbritannia.com/

Are you attending?
Yes No Maybe

Games Britannia 2012 builds on the enormous success of this year’s event, hosted at Brinsworth Comprehensive School Academy Trust, which won the Education and Technology Conference 2011 award for the “Best use of ICT to support learning in a secondary school

The potential for Games Britannia is unprecedented. We estimate that over 1500 students could attend workshops, and up to 5000 members of the public for REPLAYED. Support for the event is growing, with BAFTA and Young Game Designers, David Braben's exciting Raspberry Pi computer project and Sheffield's own SUMO Digital having confirmed attendance
